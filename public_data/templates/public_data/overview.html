Open Humans Public Data Sharing

Overview of Enrollment

Welcome to the enrollment for the Open Humans: Public Data Sharing study! Enrolling in this study will allow you to publicly share data sets you have imported to Open Humans, turning it into a public resource available to researchers, citizen scientists, and any others that may wish to learn from it and build upon it.

This is an ongoing study with no planned endpoint.

Requirements

To enroll in this study, you must:

  • be a United States citizen or permanent resident
    As with the Open Humans site as a whole, this study follows United States regulations for data management and the conduct of human subjects research. As such, only citizens and permanent residents of the United States are eligible participants.
  • be at least 18 years of age
    As described in our informed consent process, sharing data publicly could involve risks. To legally make this decision, you must be at least 18 years of age. This decision must be made by the participant: a child may not be enrolled by a parent or legal guardian.
  • enroll yourself
    Adults that are not capable of giving informed consent may not be enrolled by a legal guardian.
  • read the informed consent
    Our informed consent document is designed to be informative and readable. It is very important that you take the time to read this.
  • pass our enrollment quiz
    You must complete a brief quiz to demonstrate your understanding of this study. This quiz should take only a few minutes of your time, and helps assure us that you understand the study.
  • consent to participate
    Once you pass the quiz, we'll invite you to sign the consent form. This legal document will require you to sign your name, which will be confidential.
