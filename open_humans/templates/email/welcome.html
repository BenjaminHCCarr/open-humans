Thank you for joining the Open Humans community!

It's our mission to help connect you and your data with researchers. In Open Humans, you'll be able to build a profile with data you receive from studies and commercial sources. We'll invite you to share that data in various ways, publicly or directly with projects.

To make sure you get future emails from us, add our email to your "contacts". Without this step our emails might be "filtered" by your email client!

Add Your Data

We have many data sources you can add! Each source you add gets you a badge on your public profile. It also helps enable more studies – researchers can design studies more easily when they know members have data to ready to share.

Public Data Sharing

Data is private by default. For each data source, you can opt to turn on public sharing. To enable this feature, you'll need to join our study: {{ public_sharing_url }}

You'll be able to turn "public" status on (and off) for each of your data sources on your research data management page: {{ research_data_management_url }}
