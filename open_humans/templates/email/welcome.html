We believe research should go both ways: find studies to join and share data with, and studies that will share data with you. Build a profile that connects data across studies!

You can even choose to make some data sets public. Joining our Public Sharing Data Study turns on the “public sharing” feature. You’ll be able to turn “public” status on or off for each of your data sets.

Get started by visiting our Welcome page.

With your help, we’ll start breaking down data silos in human health and research.
