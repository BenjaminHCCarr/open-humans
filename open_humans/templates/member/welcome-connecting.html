Connecting your study accounts

If you're enrolled in a research partner's study, that means you have an account on their website. If you'd like us to import data from the study you'll need to connect these accounts. You'll also get a badge on your public profile sharing your contribution to science!

Unless otherwise noted, once you connect we'll import your study data (if it exists) and we'll automatically import future data from the study as it becomes available.

Note: You might want to open these connections in a new tab! Each connection process will leave you on the associated study's website.
