Job Posting: Executive Director, Open Humans Foundation

Position Description and Core Responsibilities

Open Humans Foundation is seeking an energetic and experienced leader to sustain and grow our organization. Reporting to the Board of Directors, the Executive Director is responsible for the programmatic and financial health and stability of the organization – and its further development. The ED provides oversight to programs, projects, and partnerships and will work closely with the Board in the areas of strategy, fundraising, and financial management. The ED is the primary point of contact in the community and will represent the values and mission of OHF. The ED should share our vision – and also have the background and ability to extend it.

The ideal candidate will be a skilled communicator comfortable in the role of chief fundraiser and leader of OHF. They will have experience in a senior management position in a small organization with direct supervisory responsibility for administration, program, budget, and fundraising, preferably in an organization focused on medical science. The candidate will have expertise in user-engagement strategy and a demonstrable belief in the power of data to generate meaning and further scientific progress. The candidate must demonstrate the ability to lead with transparency, to balance teamwork with autonomy and to make hard decisions.

Fundraising:

  • Cultivate and maintain major donors
  • Execute creative campaigns to increase individual and institutional contributions
  • Seek grant-based resources

Administration:

  • Ensure that existing grants are well-managed and deliverables are met
  • Supervise the team and grow it
  • Build and maintain relationships with important partners
  • Oversee the communications strategy for OHF.
  • Lead the strategy with the guidance and support of the board.

Program:

  • Set forth the vision of programming, based on the mission of the organization and the needs of its several communities – individual data providers, researchers, funders, policy makers
  • Ensure that programmatic goals are aligned with available resources
  • Monitor the delivery of the data platform and APIs and other services of the organization (GET Conference, PGP Network)

Work Schedule: fulltime and dedicated

About Open Humans Foundation

The Open Humans Foundation supports the vision of humans having access to their own data and sharing it at their own option with others for the benefit of science and humanity.

OHF is a small and dynamic research and citizen participation organization with an 8-year history of dramatic change from a small, 10-person public-genome project to a much broader platform for citizen participation for data collection and sharing. We collect and share microbiomes, trait data and other health data in addition to genomes. Over the past eight years we have fostered the creation of the PGP Network, the Genomes Environment Traits Conference, and the Open Humans Project.

The organization is now ready to scale, and is looking for an executive director who can help us to do so. We plan to reach a much broader data-supplying public as well as more researchers. We intend to become an even stronger voice advocating for user access to data with the option of sharing it with researchers and/or the public at large, at their option.
