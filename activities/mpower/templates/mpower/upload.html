About mPower

The mPower study is an iPhone app for monitoring the symptoms of Parkinson's disease (PD). The study uses a combination of surveys, tasks, and sensor data to investigate and track symptoms. Contributions from people without Parkinsons' disease are welcomed: this "control" data helps researchers understand what data is "normal" and what reflects the symptoms of Parkinson's disease. Visit the mPower website to learn more: http://parkinsonmpower.org
